Auto-journal watches
Watched entities auto-log a journal event when their recorded state changes - point them at crop steering phase sensors and alert sensors. Entity must also be in the recorded list on the Data tab.
Weeks are relative to flip: -2 and -1 are veg, week 1 is the first flower week. Blank cells mean no target that week.
Builds also run automatically each night at 00:15. A bundle is the grow, journal, recipe, photos, series export, and current timelapses in one zip - import someone else's and replay against it.
InfluxDB
Point this at the InfluxDB app from the add-on store, or any InfluxDB container. v1 needs username and password, v2 needs org and token. Provision creates GrowScope's own database and touches nothing else.
Recorded entities
One entity id per line, recorded every 60 seconds - numbers as values, everything else (phases, valve states) as strings. Backfill pulls whatever raw history HA still holds (about 10 days stock) so charts are not empty on day one.
Immich
Phone photos, hands free: album per grow on your Immich server, sync pulls new ones onto the timeline with their real capture times.
